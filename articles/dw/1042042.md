[联邦调查局截获输油管黑客所得赎金](https://www.dw.com/zh/%E8%81%94%E9%82%A6%E8%B0%83%E6%9F%A5%E5%B1%80%E6%88%AA%E8%8E%B7%E8%BE%93%E6%B2%B9%E7%AE%A1%E9%BB%91%E5%AE%A2%E6%89%80%E5%BE%97%E8%B5%8E%E9%87%91%C2%A0%C2%A0%C2%A0/a-57815296)
