[许章润：就女子羈獄  致暴政書](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/2020/09/%e8%ae%b8%e7%ab%a0%e6%b6%a6%ef%bc%9a%e5%b0%b1%e5%a5%b3%e5%ad%90%e7%be%88%e7%8d%84-%e8%87%b4%e6%9a%b4%e6%94%bf%e6%9b%b8/)
