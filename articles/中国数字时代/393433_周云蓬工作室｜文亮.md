晚上吃了心心念了一天的炸鸡腿，正新这次真是惨败叫鸡，感觉还是没有徐大Sao做的香啊，新的一岁希望能做一个简单的人，看得清世间繁杂却不在心中留下痕迹，保持足够的平常心。</p><p><strong>Blog of Dr. Li Wenliang, 12OCT, 2019</strong></p><p>Today is another busy day, and I could not help being impatient as I am getting more patients. Thanks to all of the patients for their understanding. The massage at noon made me feel much better, and the cervical spondylosis that has been troubling me for many days is finally gone .</p><p>The fall in Wuhan has a tenderness that is neither hot nor cold. In this season, you can find the lightest drizzle and the gentlest wind, and you will feel the beauty and rhythm of the crackling sound when you step on the fallen leaves.</p><p>I had fried chicken for dinner after thinking about it for the whole day, Zhengxin lost badly to jiaoji this time, I feel like it’s still not as delicious as the one cooked by Xu Da-sao .</p><p>In this new year I hope to be a simpler person who has a clear understanding of the complicated world, but does not leave a trace of it in their soul, and who maintains enough normalcy.</p><hr /><p style="text-align: center"><em><strong>关于这首歌</strong></em></p><p>这一年，大家过得都不容易。</p><p>李文亮医生殉职后，我有时会翻看他的微博。三月的一个清晨，因为一直想着李医生而倍感压抑的我，大声唱出了一句：世界需要不同的声音。当天晚上，这首歌就写完了。</p><p>我站在母亲床边，唱给病重的她听，母亲流泪。</p><p>六月，母亲病逝。她的早逝多少与疫情期间各种治疗上的不便有关。从前，我是一个内心充满幸福的人。母亲去世后，我的心底永远沉淀着悲伤。</p><p>七月，我问周云蓬这首歌是否应该录一下。他说：写得很好啊，录吧。</p><p>生者固然要继续前行，逝者，我也不想忘记。</p><p>在筹划录音以及录音期间，我请教和打扰了众多朋友，在此感谢：小木、周甜、张蔚、杨梓文祺、谭力波、张佺、刘东明、李志、安娜伊思·马田、张玮玮、小河、唐拉拉、彭禹、王小山、邱磊。</p><p>感谢制作人李强。</p><p>感谢周云蓬、郭龙参与合唱，你们宽厚温暖的声音为这首歌增添了力量。</p><p>感谢我的侄女王与同帮我润色英文译文以及设计歌曲封面。</p><p>感谢北京“奕星宇音乐”星芒录音棚、大理“窄门Studio”协助录音。</p><p>最后特别感谢左小祖咒对制作的帮助。</p><p style="text-align: center"><em>大方</em></p><p style="text-align: center">2020年8月24日</p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><em>About This Song</em></p><p>This year, was hard for everyone.</p><p>After Dr. Li Wen-liang passed away in the line of duty, I sometimes read his blog. In a early morning in March, I felt very depressed about Dr. Li and cried out: “The world needs different voices.” That night, I finished this song.</p><p>I sat by the bed of my mother, who was seriously ill then , and sang to her; she teared up.</p><p>In June, my mother passed away. Her death was somehow due to the unconvinence in treatment caused by COVID-19. Before then, I was a person with happiness in heart, but after, my heart was always filled with sadness.</p><p>In July, I asked Zhou Yun-peng whether I should record this song or not, he replied: “this song is very great, go to record it.”</p><p>The living have to go further, and as for the departed, I do not want to forget them.</p><p>During the planning of recording and recording, I bothered and got suggestion from many friends, thanks alot: Xiao Mu, Zhou Tian, Zhang Wei, Yang Zi Wen Qi, Tan Li-bo, Zhang Quan, Liu Dong-ming, Li Zhi, Anais Martane, Zhang Wei-wei, Xiao He, Tang La-la, Peng Yu, Wang Xiao-shan, Qiu Lei.</p><p>Thanks to producer Li Qiang.</p><p>Thanks to Zhou Yun-peng and Guo Long, your openness and warm voices increased the strength of the song.</p><p>Thanks to my niece Grace Yutong Wang for helping me polish the English translation and design the cover of the song.</p><p>Thanks to Beijing Yi Xing Yu Asterism Studio, Dali Narrow Gate Studio help recording.</p><p>At
