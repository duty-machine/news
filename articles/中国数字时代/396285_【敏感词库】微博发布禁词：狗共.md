[【敏感词库】微博发布禁词：狗共](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/2020/08/%e3%80%90%e6%95%8f%e6%84%9f%e8%af%8d%e5%ba%93%e3%80%91%e5%be%ae%e5%8d%9a%e5%8f%91%e5%b8%83%e7%a6%81%e8%af%8d%ef%bc%9a%e7%8b%97%e5%85%b1/)
