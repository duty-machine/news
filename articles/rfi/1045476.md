[面对追问 特朗普称正考虑禁止更多的中国科技企业](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E7%BB%8F%E8%B4%B8/20200816-%E9%9D%A2%E5%AF%B9%E8%BF%BD%E9%97%AE-%E7%89%B9%E6%9C%97%E6%99%AE%E7%A7%B0%E6%AD%A3%E8%80%83%E8%99%91%E7%A6%81%E6%AD%A2%E6%9B%B4%E5%A4%9A%E7%9A%84%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E7%A7%91%E6%8A%80%E4%BC%81%E4%B8%9A)
