[法德及欧洲领导人呼吁普京向卢卡申科施压](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20200818-%E6%B3%95%E5%BE%B7%E5%8F%8A%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2%E9%A2%86%E5%AF%BC%E4%BA%BA%E5%91%BC%E5%90%81%E6%99%AE%E4%BA%AC%E5%90%91%E5%8D%A2%E5%8D%A1%E7%94%B3%E7%A7%91%E6%96%BD%E5%8E%8B)
