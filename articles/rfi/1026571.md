[塞内加尔议会需两周内批准选举法案](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20210708-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-s%C3%A9n%C3%A9gal-afrique-%E5%A1%9E%E5%86%85%E5%8A%A0%E5%B0%94%E8%AE%AE%E4%BC%9A%E9%9C%80%E4%B8%A4%E5%91%A8%E5%86%85%E6%89%B9%E5%87%86%E9%80%89%E4%B8%BE%E6%B3%95%E6%A1%88)
