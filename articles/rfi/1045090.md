[特朗普或称与中国全脱钩 不跟中国做生意](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20200823-%E7%89%B9%E6%9C%97%E6%99%AE%E6%88%96%E7%A7%B0%E4%B8%8E%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%85%A8%E8%84%B1%E9%92%A9-%E4%B8%8D%E8%B7%9F%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%81%9A%E7%94%9F%E6%84%8F)
