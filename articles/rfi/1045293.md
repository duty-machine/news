[哈里里遇刺案宣判 案情仍有许多朦胧](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20200819-rfi-nicolas-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E5%93%88%E9%87%8C%E9%87%8C%E9%81%87%E5%88%BA%E6%A1%88%E5%AE%A3%E5%88%A4-%E6%A1%88%E6%83%85%E4%BB%8D%E6%9C%89%E8%AE%B8%E5%A4%9A%E6%9C%A6%E8%83%A7)
