[中共百年党庆慌什么：京沪快递切断 网民抱怨](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20210621-%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%99%BE%E5%B9%B4%E5%85%9A%E5%BA%86%E4%B9%A0%E8%BF%91%E5%B9%B3%E6%80%95%E5%95%A5-%E4%BA%AC%E6%B2%AA%E5%BF%AB%E9%80%92%E5%88%87%E6%96%AD-%E7%BD%91%E6%B0%91%E6%8A%B1%E6%80%A8)
