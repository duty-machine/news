[中国CNMC与刚果 (金)的铜矿开采协议](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20200812-rfi-nicolas-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BDcnmc%E4%B8%8E%E5%88%9A%E6%9E%9C-%E9%87%91-%E7%9A%84%E9%93%9C%E7%9F%BF%E5%BC%80%E9%87%87%E5%8D%8F%E8%AE%AE)
