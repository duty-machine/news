[秘鲁2021总统选举点票结束 悬念依旧](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20210616-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-p%C3%A9rou-election-%E7%A7%98%E9%B2%812021%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%9F%E9%80%89%E4%B8%BE%E7%82%B9%E7%A5%A8%E7%BB%93%E6%9D%9F-%E6%82%AC%E5%BF%B5%E4%BE%9D%E6%97%A7)
