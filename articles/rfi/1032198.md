[美参院提出对抗中国法案 呼吁强化与台湾伙伴关系](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20210408-%E7%BE%8E%E5%8F%82%E9%99%A2%E6%8F%90%E5%87%BA%E5%AF%B9%E6%8A%97%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%B3%95%E6%A1%88-%E5%91%BC%E5%90%81%E5%BC%BA%E5%8C%96%E4%B8%8E%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BC%99%E4%BC%B4%E5%85%B3%E7%B3%BB)
