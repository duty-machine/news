[【时代的一粒沙】为挽救濒临倒闭的奶茶店 女老板开色情直播还房租](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/2020/12/%e3%80%90%e6%97%b6%e4%bb%a3%e7%9a%84%e4%b8%80%e7%b2%92%e6%b2%99%e3%80%91%e4%b8%ba%e6%8c%bd%e6%95%91%e6%bf%92%e4%b8%b4%e5%80%92%e9%97%ad%e7%9a%84%e5%a5%b6%e8%8c%b6%e5%ba%97-%e5%a5%b3%e8%80%81%e6%9d%bf/)

class="aligncenter"
