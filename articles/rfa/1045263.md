[军人武装待命 “七一”中共党庆如临大敌   访民形容 “空前严厉”](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/ql1-06222021061149.html)
------

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日是中共一百周年党庆日，本周日（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">20</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日）起，北京众多异议人士被软禁或强制旅游，天安门广场一带草木皆兵，有网民拍摄到两百多辆满载军人的大巴进入鸟巢体育场。当地居民说这次戒备乃中共建政以来所罕见。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">距离中共百周年党庆日不足</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">10</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小时有人值班。六四时，从</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">31</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日上岗</span><span style="font-weight:
