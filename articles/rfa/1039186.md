[美台21世纪倡议协议 美参众议院通过 待拜登签署生效](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/hx1-07192023063440.html)
------

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">美国联邦参议院以“一致同意”方式通过《美台</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">21</span><span style="font-weight: style="font-weight: 400;">21</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">世纪贸易倡议首批协定执行法案》（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">US-Taiwan Initiative on 21st-Century Trade First Agreement Implementation Act</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）。将提交总统拜登（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Joe Biden</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）签署生效。</span></p><p><b>AIT：具标志性意义</b>
