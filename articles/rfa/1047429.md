[深圳倡议民众储备应急物资    民众联想到台海紧张气氛](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/zhengzhi/QL2-10162020054746.html)
------

<p><span id="docs-internal-guid-c57610d7-7fff-1bbc-cdb2-5c435dcad099"> </span></p><p dir="ltr"> </p><p
