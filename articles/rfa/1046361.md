[中印边境冲突8个月后       中国承认4名解放军阵亡](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/junshiwaijiao/ql2-02192021043304.html)
------

style="font-weight: 400;">“</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">烈士褒扬令</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，大致内容是</span><span style="font-weight:
