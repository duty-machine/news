[黎智英被指控欺诈罪遭收押，法官拒绝其保释](https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20201203/jimmy-lai-hong-kong/)
------

<div><i>王霜舟<br>2020-12-03 14:23:27</i></div><img src="https://images.weserv.nl?url=static01.nyt.com/images/2020/12/03/world/03hongkong01/merlin_175606536_68cb9013-4eb4-4091-8346-bb5214331264-articleLarge.jpg" width="100%"><div><small style="color: #999;">黎智英在8月被捕后回到《苹果日报》新闻编辑室。</small></div><p>周四，香港最大的亲民主报纸创始人黎智英因欺诈指控被拒绝保释，法院下令将其监禁至明年4月。</p><p>在72岁的黎智英被拘留的前一天，<a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20201202/joshua-wong-agnes-chow-hong-kong/" title="Link: https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20201202/joshua-wong-agnes-chow-hong-kong/">香港三名主要活动人士因去年参加抗议活动而被判刑</a>，这是香港亲民主运动受到的最新打击。</p><p>6月底，中国政府对香港实施了一项全面的国家安全法。<a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/09/world/asia/hong-kong-arrests-lai-national-security-law.html" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/09/world/asia/hong-kong-arrests-lai-national-security-law.html">8月，黎智英和他的两个儿子以及他旗下的媒体公司</a><a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/09/world/asia/hong-kong-arrests-lai-national-security-law.html" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/09/world/asia/hong-kong-arrests-lai-national-security-law.html">壹传媒(</a><a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/09/world/asia/hong-kong-arrests-lai-national-security-law.html" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2020/08/09/world/asia/hong-kong-arrests-lai-national-security-law.html">Next Digital)的四名高管被逮捕</a>，他成为国安法下最引人注目的目标。</p><p>但新的欺诈指控与国安法无关。相反，<a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="https://news.rthk.hk/rthk/en/component/k2/1563348-20201203.htm" title="Link: https://news.rthk.hk/rthk/en/component/k2/1563348-20201203.htm">据香港电台(RTHK)报道</a>，他们指责黎智英违反了壹传媒总部的租约条款。</p>
