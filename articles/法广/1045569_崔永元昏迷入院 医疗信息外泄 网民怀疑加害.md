[崔永元昏迷入院 医疗信息外泄 网民怀疑加害](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20200814-%E5%B4%94%E6%B0%B8%E5%85%83%E6%98%8F%E8%BF%B7%E5%85%A5%E9%99%A2-%E5%8C%BB%E7%96%97%E4%BF%A1%E6%81%AF%E5%A4%96%E6%B3%84-%E7%BD%91%E6%B0%91%E6%80%80%E7%96%91%E5%8A%A0%E5%AE%B3)
