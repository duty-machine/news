[Covid-19 : 瑞士人对收集个人数据争议不大](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A/20200826-rfi-nicolas-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-coronavirus-%E7%91%9E%E5%A3%AB%E4%BA%BA%E5%AF%B9%E6%94%B6%E9%9B%86%E4%B8%AA%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE%E4%BA%89%E8%AE%AE%E4%B8%8D%E5%A4%A7)
