[法国维族人举行示威声援在新疆遭迫害的家人](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD/20200817-%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E7%BB%B4%E6%97%8F%E4%BA%BA%E4%B8%BE%E8%A1%8C%E7%A4%BA%E5%A8%81%E5%A3%B0%E6%8F%B4%E5%9C%A8%E6%96%B0%E7%96%86%E9%81%AD%E8%BF%AB%E5%AE%B3%E7%9A%84%E5%AE%B6%E4%BA%BA)
