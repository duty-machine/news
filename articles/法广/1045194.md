[许胜雄暂代海基会张忠谋施振荣是新任董事长考虑人选](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E6%B8%AF%E6%BE%B3%E5%8F%B0/20200821-%E8%AE%B8%E8%83%9C%E9%9B%84%E6%9A%82%E4%BB%A3%E6%B5%B7%E5%9F%BA%E4%BC%9A%E5%BC%A0%E5%BF%A0%E8%B0%8B%E6%96%BD%E6%8C%AF%E8%8D%A3%E6%98%AF%E6%96%B0%E4%BB%BB%E8%91%A3%E4%BA%8B%E9%95%BF%E8%80%83%E8%99%91%E4%BA%BA%E9%80%89)
