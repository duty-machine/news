[前驻美大使周文重告诫美国：不应把中国视为第二个苏联](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20200823-%E5%89%8D%E9%A9%BB%E7%BE%8E%E5%A4%A7%E4%BD%BF%E5%91%A8%E6%96%87%E9%87%8D%E5%91%8A%E8%AF%AB%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD-%E4%B8%8D%E5%BA%94%E6%8A%8A%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E8%A7%86%E4%B8%BA%E7%AC%AC%E4%BA%8C%E4%B8%AA%E8%8B%8F%E8%81%94)
