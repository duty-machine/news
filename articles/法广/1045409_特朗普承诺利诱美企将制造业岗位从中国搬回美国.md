[特朗普承诺利诱美企将制造业岗位从中国搬回美国](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E7%BB%8F%E8%B4%B8/20200818-%E7%89%B9%E6%9C%97%E6%99%AE%E6%89%BF%E8%AF%BA%E5%88%A9%E8%AF%B1%E7%BE%8E%E4%BC%81%E5%B0%86%E5%88%B6%E9%80%A0%E4%B8%9A%E5%B2%97%E4%BD%8D%E4%BB%8E%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%90%AC%E5%9B%9E%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD)
