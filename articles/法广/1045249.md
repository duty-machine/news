[美国总统选举厚幕再开大 民主党竞选成型直轰特朗普](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20200820-%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%9F%E9%80%89%E4%B8%BE%E5%8E%9A%E5%B9%95%E5%86%8D%E5%BC%80%E5%A4%A7-%E6%B0%91%E4%B8%BB%E5%85%9A%E7%AB%9E%E9%80%89%E6%88%90%E5%9E%8B%E7%9B%B4%E8%BD%B0%E7%89%B9%E6%9C%97%E6%99%AE)
