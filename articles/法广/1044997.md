[恐全民检测反应冷淡累中央没面子林郑迁怒唱反调专家](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20200825-%E6%81%90%E5%85%A8%E6%B0%91%E6%A3%80%E6%B5%8B%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94%E5%86%B7%E6%B7%A1%E7%B4%AF%E4%B8%AD%E5%A4%AE%E6%B2%A1%E9%9D%A2%E5%AD%90%E6%9E%97%E9%83%91%E8%BF%81%E6%80%92%E5%94%B1%E5%8F%8D%E8%B0%83%E4%B8%93%E5%AE%B6)
